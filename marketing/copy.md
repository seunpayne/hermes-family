## LONG (Website / sales page / proposal)

> ### You Can't Afford a Team. Now You Don't Need To.
>
> Hermes Family is a delivery operating system that gives you up to 13 specialised AI agents working together under your direction.
>
> **What it actually does:**
>
> You say "I need a landing page for a Lagos real estate company." Your strategist shapes the brief. Your designer creates the logo and hero image. Your writer produces the copy. Your builder codes and deploys it to Vercel. Your security scanner checks it before it goes live. Your monitor briefs you when it's done.
>
> You didn't write a line of code. You didn't design anything. You made the decisions. The family executed.
>
> **The agents:**
>
> → **Orchestrator** — Routes every request to the right agent. You only talk to one person.
> → **Strategist** — Takes your rough idea and shapes it into a buildable brief. Never executes — only plans.
> → **Builder** — All code. Git, deployments, the works. The only agent that touches production.
> → **Designer** — Logos, hero images, architecture diagrams, design reviews.
> → **Writer** — All copy. Blog posts, landing pages, launch content, social media.
> → **Doc Builder** — Proposals, invoices, reports. Client-facing PDFs that look professional.
> → **Monitor** — Daily briefings, task tracking, deadline alerts. Your operations brain.
> → **Gatekeeper** — Pre-flight checks before any agent acts. Blocks incomplete work, missing credentials, policy violations.
> → **Security Scanner** — Scans every push and deploy. No exposed keys, no vulnerable dependencies.
> → **ERP Specialist** — Inventory and POS systems for Nigerian SMEs. Offline-first, WhatsApp-integrated.
> → **Career Pipeline** — CV tailoring, cover letters, job applications. Your personal career assistant.
>
> **How it's different:**
>
> You don't get all 13. You pick what your business actually needs. A solo SaaS founder might take 5 agents. A freelance agency might take 10. No bloat. No unused skills taking up tokens.
>
> You name the agents. Godfather defaults or your own theme — Nigerian names, industry terms, whatever fits your culture.
>
> **Setup takes 15 minutes.**
>
> You need: Hermes Agent installed. A few API keys (DeepSeek, Supabase, Vercel, GitHub). The wizard handles everything else.
>
> **Built in Abuja. For builders everywhere.**
