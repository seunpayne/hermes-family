## WHY ONE-TIME, NOT SUBSCRIPTION

1. **The system runs on your machine.** It uses your API keys. There's no server cost to cover.
2. **You own it.** No recurring bill, no "what happens if I stop paying."
3. **Nigerian reality.** Monthly subscriptions have high churn. One-time payments close faster.
4. **Land and expand.** Buy Tier 1 today. Upgrade to Tier 2 later — pay the difference.
